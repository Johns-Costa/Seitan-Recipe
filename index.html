How to make basic seitan

A simple cruelty free juicy steak that everyone will love

ingredients

  • 1 cup vital wheat gluten
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup vegetable broth or water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce or tamari
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

preparation

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the vital wheat gluten, nutritional yeast, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and salt. Mix well to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the vegetable broth or water, soy sauce or tamari, and olive oil.
  3. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for a few minutes until it becomes elastic and firm.
  5. Let the dough rest for about 10 minutes to allow the gluten to develop.
  6. Meanwhile, prepare a large pot with enough water to submerge the seitan. You can also add vegetable broth or seasonings to the water for additional flavor.
  7. Once the dough has rested, shape it into a log or desired shape. You can also tear or cut it into smaller pieces for a different texture.
  8. Place the seitan in the pot of simmering water or broth. Make sure the water is not boiling as it can make the seitan tough.
  9. Simmer the seitan for about 1 hour, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking. The seitan should double in size.
  10. Remove the seitan from the water and let it cool. You can now use it in various recipes, such as stir-fries, sandwiches, stews, or grilled dishes.
  11. Enjoy your homemade seitan!
    12. Note: The cooking time may vary depending on the size and thickness of the seitan. You can experiment with different seasonings and spices to customize the flavor to your liking.
